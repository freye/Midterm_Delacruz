PLACES TO VISIT IN MAMBAJAO CITY

Medano Island

Popularly known as Whit Island, it is a long strip of velvety -soft sand bar hemmed by very clean and clear turquoise water.

Mt. Hibok Hibok

An active volcano, Mt. Hibok-Hibok rises about 4,370 feet above sea level. It is rich with endemic flora and fauna.

Plaza Union

Commonly referred to as the 1914 Historical Monument, the National Commission on Culture and the Arts lists Plaza Union as among the countryâ€™s National Important Cultural Properties.

Ardent Spring Resort

Tucked in the woods of Barangay Tagdo, Ardent Spring Resort is hideway of huge rock pools filled with natural warm water cascading from the mountain. It is perfect for lazy wallows after a long day of tour.

Tongatok Cliff Viewdeck

With the fantastic view of the sea and Mt. Vulcan in the backdrop, Tongatok Cliff surely makes it the best scene for your insta-perfect photos.

Katibawasan Falls

Falling about 70 meters high, Katibawasan Falls make it the highest waterfall in Camiguin Island. Take a dip and enjoy its freezing cold water.
